I do something like:

1. Skim material, all of it, to get an idea of scope. Don't focus on learning / memorizing, etc. This is just a vibe check.
1. Go back over material, slower, with an eye towards the exam topics.
1. Take basic notes.
1. Pick a topic, create a lab with a working example.
1. Find source materials, or more examples of a given technology.
1. Talk with others about the topic.
1. Understand and break the existing lab, (look at fundamentals, like debugs, logs, packets.
1. Take more advanced notes documenting observations about behavior. Read source materials.
1. Understand where this concept fits in with the rest of the related concepts ...

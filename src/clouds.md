# Cloud Terms
* **IaaS:** Infrastructure as a Service. Instead of buying routers, switches, or servers, you can rent digital versions, online.

IaaS Providers: AWS (Amazon), Azure (Microsoft), Google Cloud Platform.

* **SaaS:** Software as a service. Instead of owning or installing software, you can rent it, online.

SaaS Options: Microsoft 365 (MS Teams), Salesforce (CRM), Atlassian (Agile), Adobe Creative Cloud

* **Private Cloud:** Equipment you own, on a network you own, to reach services you own. Formerly called an Intranet.

* **[Colo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colocation_centre):** Putting equipment you own into a datacenter ... because it's safe there.

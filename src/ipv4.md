# IPv4

The [2004 Turing award] was given to [Vinton Cerf] and [Robert Kahn] for the invention of [TCP/IP].

[TCP/IP]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_protocol_suite
[Vinton Cerf]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vint_Cerf
[Robert Kahn]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Kahn_(computer_scientist)
[2004 Turing award]: https://amturing.acm.org/byyear.cfm

Concepts from the 1974 IEEE paper:

- Gateways
  - Separate different L2 networks
- TCP Port Multiplexing
- Connection Oriented
- Sequence Numbers
- Flow Control
- Retransmission
